=== ANNUAL LEAVE POLICY ===

All full-time employees receive 25 days of paid annual leave per calendar year, prorated for mid-year joiners.

Leave Entitlement by Location:
- Cyberjaya (Malaysia): 25 days annual + 11 public holidays
- Labuan (Malaysia): 25 days annual + 11 public holidays
- Dubai (UAE): 25 days annual + 10 public holidays (Islamic holidays vary by moon sighting)
- Malta (EU): 25 days annual + 14 public holidays

Carry-Over: Up to 5 unused days may be carried to the next year. Unused days beyond 5 expire on March 31.
Encashment: Not permitted. Employees are encouraged to take their full entitlement.
Approval: Leave requests require direct manager approval via the HRIS at least 5 business days in advance. Requests over 5 consecutive days require 2 weeks notice.
Half-Days: Half-day leave is permitted. Minimum leave unit is half a day.

Sick Leave:
- All locations: 14 days paid sick leave per year.
- Medical certificate required for absences of 3+ consecutive days.
- Malaysia: Additional 60 days hospitalization leave per Employment Act.

Parental Leave:
- Maternity: 90 days paid (all locations). UAE: 60 days full pay + 15 half pay. Malaysia: 98 days per Employment Act.
- Paternity: 10 days paid (all locations).
- Adoption: 60 days paid leave for primary caregiver.

Compassionate Leave: 3 days for death of immediate family member (parent, spouse, child, sibling).

=== EXPENSE POLICY ===

Eligible Expenses:
- Business travel (flights, hotels, ground transport)
- Client entertainment (pre-approved, max $150/person)
- Professional development (conferences, courses — up to $2,000/year with manager approval)
- Coworking space: Up to $300/month for remote employees with approved remote work agreement
- Home office equipment: One-time $500 setup allowance for new hires
- Mobile phone: $50/month reimbursement for roles requiring constant availability
- Gym/wellness: Up to $100/month under Wellness Benefit (receipts required)

Submission Process:
- Submit via Concur expense system within 30 days of expense
- All receipts must be attached digitally
- Expenses over $500 require pre-approval from department head
- International expenses: Convert to USD at date-of-transaction rate

Per Diem Rates (Business Travel):
- Cyberjaya: $60/day
- Dubai: $100/day
- Malta: $80/day
- Labuan: $50/day
- Other locations: Refer to company travel policy on intranet

Non-Eligible Expenses:
- Personal travel upgrades
- Alcohol (unless part of approved client entertainment)
- Personal subscriptions
- Parking fines or traffic violations

=== BENEFITS ===

Health Insurance:
- All employees and dependents covered under company group health plan
- Coverage begins on first day of employment
- Includes: medical, dental, vision, mental health counseling (12 sessions/year)
- Dependents: Spouse and children under 21 (under 25 if full-time students)

Life Insurance: 4x annual salary, company-paid.

Retirement/Pension:
- Malaysia (EPF): Company contributes 13% of salary (statutory minimum 12% + 1% additional)
- UAE: End of Service Gratuity per UAE Labour Law (21 days salary per year for first 5 years, 30 days after)
- Malta: Company contributes 10% to private pension scheme
- All locations: Optional salary sacrifice pension top-up available

Employee Stock Options:
- Eligible after 1 year of employment
- Standard grant: Varies by level and role
- Vesting: 4-year schedule with 1-year cliff (25% vests after year 1, then monthly)
- Exercise window: 90 days after departure

Wellness Benefits:
- $100/month gym/fitness reimbursement
- Annual health screening (company-paid)
- Mental health support: 12 free counseling sessions via EAP
- Flexible Fridays: Early finish at 4 PM on Fridays

=== PROMOTION & CAREER GROWTH ===

Promotion Eligibility:
- Minimum 12 months in current role
- Meets or exceeds performance expectations in most recent 2 review cycles
- Demonstrates competencies of the next level
- Manager recommendation required
- Calibration review by department head and HR

Review Cycles:
- Performance reviews: Semi-annual (June and December)
- Promotion windows: January and July
- Compensation adjustments: Annual in January, aligned with market data

Career Development:
- $2,000 annual learning budget per employee
- Internal mobility: Employees may apply for internal roles after 12 months
- Mentorship program available to all employees
- Leadership development track for senior individual contributors

=== REMOTE WORK POLICY ===

Hybrid Model: Employees work from office Tuesday-Thursday, remote Monday and Friday.
Fully Remote: Available for approved roles. Requires signed Remote Work Agreement.

Requirements:
- Reliable internet (minimum 50 Mbps)
- Secure, private workspace
- Available during core hours (10 AM - 4 PM local time)
- Camera on for video calls by default

Equipment: Company provides laptop. Monitor and peripherals via $500 setup allowance.
Coworking: $300/month reimbursement for approved coworking spaces.

Cross-Border Remote Work:
- Maximum 30 days per year working from a different country
- Requires pre-approval from HR (tax and visa implications)
- Not permitted from sanctioned countries

=== ONBOARDING PROCESS ===

Before Day 1:
- Offer letter signed → HR generates employment contract, NDA, equity documents
- IT provisions laptop, email, system access
- Manager assigns onboarding buddy
- Calendar invitations for first week sent automatically

Day 1:
- Welcome session with HR (company overview, policies, benefits enrollment)
- IT equipment handover and setup
- Meet with manager: 90-day goals, team intro
- Security badge and office tour

First 30 Days:
- Complete all mandatory training (Safety, Data Privacy, AML if applicable)
- 1:1 with manager weekly
- Meet key stakeholders across departments

90-Day Review:
- Manager and new hire review 90-day goals
- Feedback session
- Confirm continued employment (probation ends)

=== COMPLIANCE & LEGAL ===

Work Authorization:
- All employees must maintain valid work authorization for their location
- HR tracks visa/work permit expiries and initiates renewal 90 days before expiry
- Employees must notify HR immediately of any changes to immigration status

Certifications:
- Safety Training: Mandatory for ALL employees. Annual renewal.
- Anti-Money Laundering (AML): Mandatory for Finance and Compliance departments. Annual renewal.
- GDPR/Data Privacy: Mandatory for all employees handling personal data. Annual renewal.
- Fire Safety: Mandatory for UAE and Malta offices per local regulations.
- HR Compliance: Required for all HR department staff.

Equipment:
- Company equipment must be returned within 14 days of role change or departure
- Security tokens must be returned immediately upon expiry — unreturned tokens are a security incident
- IT equipment audited quarterly

Confidentiality:
- All employees sign NDA on hire
- Company information must not be shared externally without authorization
- Client data subject to additional handling requirements per data classification policy
