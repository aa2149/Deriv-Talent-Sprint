=== MALAYSIA (Cyberjaya / Labuan) ===

Governing Law: Employment Act 1955 (Peninsular Malaysia), Sabah Labour Ordinance (Labuan)
Language: English
Currency: MYR
Probation: 3 months (extendable to 6)
Notice Period: 1 month (during probation: 2 weeks)
EPF: Employer 13%, Employee 11%
SOCSO: Employer contribution per statutory rates
EIS: 0.2% employer, 0.2% employee
Working Hours: 45 hours/week maximum (Employment Act Amendment 2022)
Overtime: 1.5x for normal days, 2x for rest days, 3x for public holidays
Termination: Per Employment Act provisions. Retrenchment requires LIFO principle and notification to Labour Department.
Non-Compete: Generally unenforceable in Malaysia. Use non-solicitation instead (12 months max).
Annual Leave: Minimum 8 days (1-2 years), 12 days (2-5 years), 16 days (5+ years). Deriv provides 25 days for all.
Maternity: 98 consecutive days per Employment Act. Deriv provides 90 days at full pay.
Paternity: 7 days per Employment Act. Deriv provides 10 days.

=== UAE (Dubai) ===

Governing Law: UAE Federal Decree-Law No. 33 of 2021 (New Labour Law)
Language: English and Arabic (Arabic prevails in disputes)
Currency: AED
Probation: 6 months maximum
Notice Period: 30 days minimum (during probation: 14 days)
WPS: Wages Protection System compliance required
Gratuity: 21 days basic salary per year (first 5 years), 30 days per year (after 5 years)
Working Hours: 8 hours/day, 48 hours/week. Ramadan: 6 hours/day.
Overtime: 1.25x normal rate (daytime), 1.5x (nighttime 10PM-4AM)
MOHRE: Employment contract must be registered with Ministry of Human Resources and Emiratisation
Work Permit: Employer sponsors employee visa. Renewal 30-60 days before expiry.
Non-Compete: Enforceable for max 2 years if reasonable in scope and geography.
Annual Leave: 30 calendar days per UAE Labour Law (after 1 year). Deriv provides 25 working days.
Maternity: 60 days (45 full pay + 15 half pay per UAE Law). Deriv tops up to 90 days full pay.
Paternity: 5 working days per UAE Law. Deriv provides 10 days.
End of Contract: Limited vs Unlimited contracts. Deriv uses unlimited contracts.

Overtime: Per collective agreement or individual contract

Sick Leave: Per medical certificate. Employer pays first 3 days, then government Social Security.

=== EQUITY GRANT STANDARD TERMS ===

Option Type: Incentive Stock Options (where applicable) or Non-Qualified Stock Options
Vesting Schedule: 4-year monthly vesting with 1-year cliff
- Year 1: 25% vests on 1-year anniversary
- Years 2-4: Remaining 75% vests monthly (2.083% per month)
Exercise Price: Fair Market Value on grant date
Exercise Window: 90 days post-termination (extended to 12 months for good leavers with 4+ years tenure)
Accelerated Vesting: 100% on Change of Control (double-trigger)
Clawback: Options subject to clawback in cases of gross misconduct

Standard Grant Ranges by Level:
- Junior (L1-L2): 1,000 - 5,000 options
- Mid (L3-L4): 5,000 - 15,000 options
- Senior (L5-L6): 15,000 - 40,000 options
- Lead/Principal (L7+): 40,000 - 100,000 options
- Director+: Per board approval

=== NDA STANDARD TERMS ===

Duration: Confidentiality obligations survive termination indefinitely for trade secrets, 3 years for other confidential information.
Scope: All non-public company information, client data, technical know-how, business strategies, financial data.
Exclusions: Information that becomes public through no fault of employee, information independently developed, information received from third party without restriction.
Remedies: Injunctive relief + damages. Employee acknowledges monetary damages may be insufficient.
Return of Materials: All company materials (physical and digital) must be returned on termination.
